Smart Food Saver

Smart Food Saver is an AI-powered web application that helps detect the spoilage stage of fruits and vegetables and provide meaningful insights such as recipe ideas, inventory tracking, food waste reduction tracking, and overall food management.
The project aims to reduce food wastage by helping users understand what items are fresh, usable, or turning spoiled.
